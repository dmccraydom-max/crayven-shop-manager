CrayvenCreates Shop Manager

A personal dashboard tool for managing my Etsy shop

This is a private, single-use tool built to help me manage my own Etsy store, CrayvenCreates. It connects to my shop's data to consolidate analytics, listing performance, order history, and customer reviews into one organized view for my personal use.

What it does

  • Reads listing data and performance stats from my own shop
  • Tracks order history and monthly sales figures
  • Monitors customer reviews and feedback
  • Organizes product data across my digital product catalog

Important notes

  • This tool is built exclusively for managing my own Etsy store
  • It does not access, manage, or interact with any other Etsy accounts
  • No data is shared with or sold to third parties
  • This is a personal project, not a commercial product or service
